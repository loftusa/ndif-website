Get Involved

NDIF is under continuous development, and there are several ways to get involved in the prototype fabric during its construction.

  • Join our community of early adopters. Our online discord server hosts students and researchers from many fields who are dissecting the behavior and mechanisms of large AI models. Request early access to the prototype NDIF service through this community.
  • Use our open-source library, nnsight. NNsight can be used with NDIF and it can also be used with your own local computation power, independently of the fabric. It works with pytorch and can be easily installed via pip.
  • Join our team. We are looking for talented and motivated team members who are inspired to create a vibrant scientific community to crack the mysteries of large-scale AI. Jobs and opportunities are listed on this page.

The NDIF project code is open-source and can be found at github.com/ndif-team.


An NDIF workshop for undergraduate and graduate students, introducing state-of-the-art methods for performing interventions on the internal computations of large language models, using NDIF. We are seeking a dynamic and tech-savvy individual to join our team as the Technical Community Outreach & Education Manager for a cutting-edge nationally funded AI research platform around Large Language Models. As the face of our project, you will be instrumental in creating and nurturing a vibrant community of researchers. Your primary responsibilities include developing engaging content such as newsletters, articles, technical tutorials, and FAQs tailored to both technical and non-technical audiences. In addition to content creation, you will be responsible for organizing open-source efforts to create tutorials and online materials, and you will organize conferences and workshops to help foster collaboration within the community.

The ideal candidate will have a strong ability to convey complex concepts in an accessible manner and will have a strong understanding of machine learning and AI.

Your role will be pivotal in establishing our facility as the leading AI hub in America. You will drive initiatives that enhance our community's engagement and position us as a thought leader in the field. If you are passionate about technology, community building, and aspire to play a key role in shaping the future of AI research, we encourage you to apply.

This is a full-time role, based in Boston, MA.

You will be responsible for full stack web development, doing both front end and back-end changes dealing with both software and hardware issues where we deal with high volume traffic and throughput requests from researchers.
